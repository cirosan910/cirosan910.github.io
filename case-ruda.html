mobility with attitude.

Ruda entered a crowded e-bike market where most brands looked clinical and interchangeable. The challenge was to stand out without losing trust.

It needed a system that felt energetic and urban, built to scale across product, retail, and digital from day one.

// 02 / Approach

a system built to move.

I designed a flexible identity: a confident wordmark, a bold color story, and a modular layout system that works from a sticker to a storefront.

Every element was made to feel fast, clean, and unmistakably Ruda.
